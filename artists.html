Kayli Mesly

Kayli’s talent and style transcend traditional tattooing and is more evocative of fine art.

Drawing inspiration from Renaissance, romanticism, fairytale, and natural imagery,
she prides herself on interweaving her client’s contemporary requests into traditional
aesthetics to create truly timeless works.

Although a maximalist himself, Justin believes that less is more in life,

Mack Johnson

Mack's tattoo career began four years ago. She excelled in oil painting and
academic drawing from an early age, finding inspiration from art nouveau,
romanticism, and nature. Prior to tattooing, she worked in graphic design,
which has played a key part in developing her minimal yet dynamic compositions.

Mack’s designs are a mixture of realism, line work, and negative space.
She specializes in black & grey, micro-realism, and pet/wildlife portraits.

Instagram: @mack_johnson

James Williams

James's tattoo compositions draw from the same inspiration from his upbringing -
classical paintings, woodcut engravings, and sculpture work, as well as vintage 20th-century imagery.

These integrations create an appeal to the intellect through sometimes surrealistic dichotomies in his
micro-realism designs.

He strives to emphasize the influence of fine art in his work - he believes it adds a certain
sophistication to how a tattoo adorns a part of the body.
