Before Tattoo Notes

Before getting a tattoo, please keep the following notes in mind:

  • Ensure you are well-hydrated and have eaten something light before your appointment.
  • Avoid alcohol and caffeine before your tattoo session.
  • Make sure to bring a valid ID for age verification.
  • Discuss any allergies or medical conditions with your tattoo artist beforehand.

Aftercare Notes

Proper aftercare is crucial for a successful healing process. Here are our recommended aftercare tips:

  • Keep the tattooed area clean and dry.
  • Avoid touching the tattoo with dirty hands.
  • Apply a thin layer of tattoo aftercare cream as recommended by your artist.
  • Avoid direct sunlight and swimming until fully healed.
  • Refrain from scratching or picking at the tattoo.
